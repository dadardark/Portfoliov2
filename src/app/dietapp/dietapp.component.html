This was a project solely focused on the software development lifecycle (SDLC) of a mobile diet application. The focus was adhering to industry standards around writing formal documentation including SRS, Low and High-fidelity prototyping, Usability testing and handoff. We used the Maze application to conduct testing for our prototypes and gain feedback to be iterated into our final design. I used agile tools such as Gannt charts and burn up/down charts to track the progression of the project.
