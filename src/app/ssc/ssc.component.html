The Curtin sustanability Challenge 2024 was a challenge-based, intensive unit which provided me the ability to learn about vital sustainability questions in an interdisciplinary team-based approach over the course of a week. I worked closely with researchers, academic leads and industry partners such as Ampcontrol to gain real-world insight into the challenges faced by organizations seeking to incorporate sustainable practices into their operations. The challenge ended with a formal expo event and to showcase our solution, I created an interactive roadmap using Adobe XD.
